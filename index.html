About us

The short version.

HorseWorks is a small, independent team building games on Roblox. No big studio, no suits — just a handful of people who like making things that are satisfying to play.

Instead of spreading ourselves across ten half-finished ideas, we're putting everything into one game first: Pillars. If it goes well, there'll be more where that came from.

(And yes — "HorseWorks" is just a name we liked. No relation to any other Horse Works, HorseWorks, or horse-adjacent business out there.)
